# Privacy Policy for SkaterProgress

SkaterProgress respects your privacy.

This application does not require user accounts and does not collect or transmit
personal data to external servers.

All data entered into the app (such as athlete profiles, competition results,
program elements, and scores) is stored locally on the user’s device.

SkaterProgress does not use:
- advertising SDKs
- analytics services
- third-party trackers
- cloud storage or synchronization (as of version 1.0.0)

The app does not share user data with third parties.

Future versions of the app may introduce optional features such as cloud backup
or data synchronization. If such features are added, this Privacy Policy will be
updated accordingly.
